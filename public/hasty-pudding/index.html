Hasty Pudding

Home

Total cook time approx. 90 minutes

Ingredients:
  • (batter)
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup raisins
  • (sauce)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 cup water - boiling
  • 2 tsp vanilla
Instructions:
  • (batter)
  • Sift flour, sugar, cocoa, salt, and baking powder in a bowl
  • Mix in butter
  • Add in raisins and milk and mix well
  • Put in buttered baking dish and make sauce
  • (sauce)
  • Mix sugar flour, salt, and butter in a bowl
  • Add water and vanilla and stir
  • (pudding)
  • Pour sauce over batter
  • Bake in oven at 375Â°F for 45 minutes
  • Serve hot

approx. 90 minutes
