Total cook time approx. 90 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 4 cup flour
  • 1 lb shortening
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg - slightly beaten
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • cold water
Instructions:
  • Add egg and vinegar to measuring cup, then fill with water to 3/4 cup
  • Rub Shortening, baking powder, and salt in flour
  • Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients
  • Knead dough until is holds together
  • Wrap in wax paper and chill
Cook time
approx. 90 minutes
