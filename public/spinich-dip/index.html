Spinich Dip

Home

Total cook time approx. 0 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1 package Knorr onion soup mix
  • 1 can water chestnuts
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 package frozen spinach - small, thawed, chopped
  • 1 loaf French bread
Instructions:
  • Combine mix, water chestnuts, sour cream, mayonnaise, and spinach in a blender
  • Hollow-out loaf and dice contents
  • Serve dip in bread bowl
