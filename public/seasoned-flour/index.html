Seasoned Flour

Home

Total cook time approx. 5 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp celery salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp dry mustard
  • 4 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
Instructions:
  • Mix together and store in a plastic container
Cook time
approx. 5 minutes
