Creamy Chocolate Rum Truffles

Home

Total cook time approx. 90 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 3 cup semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup dark rum
  • 1/2 tsp rum extract
Instructions:
  • Melt chocolate and milk over low heat in heavy pan
  • Remove from heat and stir in rum and extract
  • !can substitute rum and extract for 1tbsp vanilla
  • Cool until firm
  • Shape into balls and chill for 1 hour
  • (can roll balls in coconut flakes, chapped nuts, sprinkles, or coloured sugar)
Cook time
approx. 90 minutes
