Japanese Cabbage Salad

Home

Total cook time approx. 30 minutes

Ingredients:
  • (salad)
  • 1/2 cup almonds - sliced, toasted
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds - toasted
  • 1/2 head medium cabbage - shredded
  • 1 package bean sprouts
  • 2 cup mushrooms - sliced
  • 2 green onions - chopped
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1 package Ichiban noodles - broken
  • 1/2 package chow mein noodles - broken
  • (dressing)
  • 1 package Ichiban seasoning
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2-4 tbsp soya
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
Instructions:
  • (salad)
  • Toast almonds and sesame seeds at 350Â°F until golden brown
  • Put cabbage and bean sprouts in a bowl
  • Add mushrooms, onions, sunflower seeds, almonds, and sesame seeds; set aside
  • Place broken chow mein noodles and Ichiban noodles into a bowl; set aside
  • (dressing)
  • Put seasoning into a bowl and add oil, vinegar, sugar, and a bit of soya
  • Add soya to taste, depending on cabbage
  • Stir in MSG, salt, and pepper
  • Mix dressing with cabbage, then add broken noodles
Cook time
approx. 30 minutes
