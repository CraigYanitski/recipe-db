Cherry Almond Nanaimo Bars

Home

Total cook time approx. 180 minutes

Ingredients:
  • (bottom)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar - granulated
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg - beaten
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 1 3/4 cup graham wafer - crushed
  • 1/2 cup almonds - chopped
  • (filling)
  • 1/4 cup butter - softened
  • 2 tbsp cherry juice
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 2 cup icing sugar
  • 1/3 cup maraschino cherries - chopped
  • (top)
  • 2 squares semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1 tbsp butter
Instructions:
  • (bottom)
  • Cook butter, sugar, cocoa, vanilla, and egg in a saucepan over low heat
  • !a double-boiler might work better
  • Stir constantly until mixture begins to thicken
  • Remove from heat and stir in coconut, wafers, and almonds
  • Pat firmly into a greased 2.5 L baking pan
  • Chill for 1 hour
  • (filling)
  • Cream butter, cherry juice, and extract
  • Gradually add icing sugar and beat until smooth
  • Mix cherries into filling
  • Spread over bottom layer
  • Chill until firm
  • (top)
  • Heat chocolate and butter until smooth
  • Drizzle over filling
  • Chill until firm
Cook time
approx. 180 minutes
