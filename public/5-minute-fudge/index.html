5-minute Fudge

Home

Total cook time approx. 10 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened condensed milk
  • 1 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup marshmallows
  • 1 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup nuts
Instructions:
  • Mix milk, sugar, and salt and bring to a boil
  • Cook for 5 minutes while stirring
  • Take of heat and add the other ingredients
  • Pour in baking sheet and cool
Cook time
approx. 10 minutes
