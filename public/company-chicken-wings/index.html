Company Chicken Wings

Home

Total cook time approx. 90 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 2 lb chicken wings
  • 2-3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4-3/4 cup soya
  • 2 tbsp ginger
  • 1 clove garlic - minced
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
Instructions:
  • Sprinkle wings with sugar and allow to sit at room temperature for 15 minutes in a small bowl
  • Add the rest of the ingredients and let sit for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally
  • Lay on flat pan and cook in oven at 350Â°F for 45 minutes
