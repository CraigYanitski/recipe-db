Rice Krispie Squares

Home

Total cook time approx. 30 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 5 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 6 cup Rice Krispies
Instructions:
  • Melt butter in large saucepan over low heat
  • !can substitute margarine for butter
  • Add marshmallows and stir until melted
  • !can substitute 40 large marshmallows for mini marshmallows
  • Stir in vanilla extract and Rice Krispies until well coated
  • Using a lightly-buttered spatula, stir into a buttered pan
  • Let cool until hard
Cook time
approx. 30 minutes
