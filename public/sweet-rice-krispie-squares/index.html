Sweet Rice Krispie Squares

Home

Total cook time approx. 240 minutes

Ingredients:
  • (base)
  • 16 oz butterscotch - chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 4 cup Rice Krispies
  • (fudge)
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 16 oz semi-sweet chocolate - chips
  • 1/2 cup icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
Instructions:
  • (base)
  • Melt butterscotch and peanut butter in a heavy saucepan over low heat
  • Stir constantly until smooth
  • Remove from heat and add Rice Krispies
  • Stir until well coated
  • Press half of mixture into a buttered pan and chill
  • (fudge)
  • Melt chocolate and butter in saucepan
  • Add sugar and water
  • Stir until smooth
  • Spread over chilled base
  • Spread remaining base mixture over fudge
  • Chill
  • Let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes before cutting
Cook time
approx. 240 minutes
