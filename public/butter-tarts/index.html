Butter Tarts

Home

Total cook time approx. 20 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup brown sugar (not garlic powder)
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup walnuts - chopped
  • 1 egg - beaten
  • 1 cup raisins
  • tart shells
Instructions:
  • Cream butter and sugar, add beaten egg then walnuts and raisins
  • Put 1 tsp in each shell
  • Bake in oven at 400°F for 8 minutes, then reduce to 350°F for 5 minutes
Cook time
approx. 20 minutes
