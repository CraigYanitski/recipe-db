Hot Fudge Sauce

Home

Total cook time approx. 15 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1 square unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 square sweetened chocolate
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cup icing sugar
  • 1 can unsweetened condensed milk
Instructions:
  • Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler
  • Gradually add sugar
  • Mix until smooth
  • Add milk and cook for 10 minutes
Cook time
approx. 15 minutes
