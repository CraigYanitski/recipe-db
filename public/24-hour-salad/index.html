24-hour Salad

Home

Total cook time approx. 1440 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs - beaten
  • 4 tbsp vinegar
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 oranges - diced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 can fruit salad (drained)
  • 2 cup marshmallow
  • 1 cup cream - whipped
Instructions:
  • Put eggs in double boiler and add vinegar and sugar, beating constantly until thinkened and smooth
  • Add butter, stir, and cool
  • When cold, fold in whipped cream and fruit mixture
  • Place in cooler for 24 hours
  • !can be made 24 hours before adding fruit mixture
Cook time
approx. 1440 minutes
