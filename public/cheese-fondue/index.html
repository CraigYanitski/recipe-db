Cheese Fondue

Home

Total cook time approx. 45 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 clove garlic - minced
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese - shredded
  • 1 can cheddar soup
  • 2 tbsp flour
Instructions:
  • Mix cheese and flour in a bowl
  • Simmer Wine and garlic in a pot
  • Add cheese and flour mixture to pot and heat until cheese melted
  • Blend in soup and heat until smooth
Cook time
approx. 45 minutes
